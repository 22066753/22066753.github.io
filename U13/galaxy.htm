Samsung Galaxy

Samsung is a South Korean company that is one of the world’s largest producers of electronic devices. Samsung first entered the electronics industry in 1969 with several electronics-focused divisions. The 2000s witnessed the birth of Samsung’s Galaxy smartphone series, which quickly not only became the company’s most-praised products but also were among the best-selling smartphones in the world. There have been 22 models of the galaxy in 2023 with the Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra.
